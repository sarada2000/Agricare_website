Bacterial Leaf Blight

A bacterial disease causing leaf wilting and yellowing in rice

Overview

Bacterial leaf blight is caused by Xanthomonas oryzae. It spreads rapidly in flooded and warm conditions.

Symptoms

  • Yellowing from leaf tips
  • Leaf drying and wilting
  • Milky bacterial ooze in severe cases

Causes

  • Bacterial infection
  • Infected seeds and water splash

Treatment & Prevention

  • Use disease-free seeds
  • Apply copper-based bactericides
  • Ensure proper drainage
