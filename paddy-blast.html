Paddy Blast

A severe fungal disease that affects leaves, nodes, and panicles

Overview

Paddy blast is caused by the fungus Magnaporthe oryzae. It is one of the most destructive rice diseases worldwide.

Symptoms

  • Diamond-shaped lesions with gray centers
  • Leaf, node, and neck blast
  • Broken panicles

Causes

  • High humidity and low temperatures
  • Excess nitrogen fertilizer

Treatment & Prevention

  • Use blast-resistant varieties
  • Apply fungicides at early stages
  • Balanced fertilizer application
