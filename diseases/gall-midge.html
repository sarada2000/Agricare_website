Gall Midge

Gall Midge

A destructive rice pest that causes abnormal shoot growth

Overview

Gall midge is an insect pest whose larvae attack rice tillers, leading to malformed growth known as silver shoots.

Symptoms

  • Silver shoot formation
  • No panicle development
  • Stunted plant growth

Causes

  • Infestation by gall midge larvae
  • High humidity conditions

Treatment & Prevention

  • Use resistant rice varieties
  • Remove affected plants early
  • Maintain proper field sanitation
