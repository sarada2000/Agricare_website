Tungro Disease

A viral disease transmitted by green leafhoppers affecting rice crops

Overview

Tungro is a serious viral disease that causes stunted growth and yellowing of rice plants.

Symptoms

  • Yellow to orange leaf discoloration
  • Stunted plant growth
  • Reduced tillering

Causes

  • Transmitted by green leafhoppers
  • Presence of infected plants

Treatment & Prevention

  • Remove infected plants
  • Control leafhopper population
  • Use resistant rice varieties
