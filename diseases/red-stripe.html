Red Stripe Disease

A bacterial disease that causes red streaks on rice leaves

Overview

Red stripe disease affects rice leaves, causing streak-like lesions that reduce photosynthesis and plant health.

Symptoms

  • Red or brown stripes along leaf veins
  • Leaf drying and wilting
  • Reduced crop vigor

Causes

  • Bacterial infection
  • Use of contaminated seeds

Treatment & Prevention

  • Use certified clean seeds
  • Apply copper-based bactericides
  • Avoid excessive irrigation
