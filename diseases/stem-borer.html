Stem Borer

An insect pest that damages rice stems and reduces yield

Overview

Stem borers are larvae that bore into rice stems, interrupting nutrient flow.

Symptoms

  • Dead hearts in vegetative stage
  • White empty panicles

Control Methods

  • Apply recommended insecticides
  • Early planting
  • Destroy crop residues
