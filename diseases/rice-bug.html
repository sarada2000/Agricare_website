Rice Bug

An insect pest that feeds on developing rice grains

Overview

Rice bugs suck sap from developing grains, leading to empty or discolored kernels and reduced grain quality.

Symptoms

  • Empty or shriveled grains
  • Brown or black grain discoloration
  • Unpleasant odor during infestation

Control Methods

  • Use light traps to attract insects
  • Apply recommended insecticides
  • Regular field monitoring

Prevention

  • Maintain clean field surroundings
  • Harvest crops on time
