Brown Spot Disease

A fungal disease that causes brown lesions on rice leaves

Overview

Brown spot is caused by the fungus Bipolaris oryzae. It mainly affects rice leaves and can significantly reduce yield.

Symptoms

  • Small brown circular spots on leaves
  • Yellow halo around lesions
  • Premature leaf drying

Causes

  • Fungal infection
  • Poor soil nutrition
  • Water stress

Treatment & Prevention

  • Apply recommended fungicides
  • Improve soil fertility
  • Use resistant varieties
