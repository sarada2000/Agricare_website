Fertilizer Plan

Recommended schedule for 3.5 Month Paddy Varieties.

3 Phases

Application Cycle

N-P-K

Core Nutrients

1
Phase 1: Basal Dressing

At Sowing / Planting

Apply this mixture to prepare the soil before you sow the seeds. This helps strong root development.

Urea: 15kg/acre TSP: 25kg/acre
2
Phase 2: Top Dressing 1

2 Weeks After Sowing

The "Vegetative Stage". The plant needs Nitrogen to grow tall and produce leaves.

Urea: 35kg/acre
3
Phase 3: Top Dressing 2

7-8 Weeks After Sowing

"Panicle Initiation". The plant prepares to produce rice grains. Potassium is crucial here.

Urea: 25kg/acre MOP: 15kg/acre

*Note: Dosages are estimates for Dry Zone. Please consult your local Agrarian Officer for exact soil testing.
