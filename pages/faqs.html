Help Center

Find answers to common questions about AgriCare.

How do I scan a leaf correctly? +

Go to the Home page and click "Scan Now." Ensure you are in a well-lit area. Hold your phone steady and take a close-up photo of the affected part of the leaf. Avoid blurry images for the best accuracy.

Is this service free for farmers? +

Yes, absolutely. AgriCare is a non-profit initiative designed to help Sri Lankan farmers. All features, including the AI scanner and Disease Library, are 100% free.

Which diseases can the AI detect? +

Our model is currently trained to detect: Brown Spot, Paddy Blast, Bacterial Leaf Blight, Tungro, and Stem Borer damage. We are constantly updating the database.

Does the app work without internet? +

The website requires an internet connection to analyze images. However, our downloadable Mobile App (coming soon) will have an offline mode for basic diagnosis in remote fields.

How accurate is the diagnosis? +

Our AI model has an accuracy rate of approximately 94.2% based on our test dataset. However, we always recommend consulting a local Agrarian Officer for final confirmation before using chemical treatments.
