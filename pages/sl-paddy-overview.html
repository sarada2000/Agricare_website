Paddy in Sri Lanka

The Backbone of Our Nation

Rice is the staple food of 22 million Sri Lankans. It is cultivated in 34% of the total cultivated area in the country. About 1.8 million farm families are engaged in paddy cultivation.

Cultivation Seasons

Season Period Rainfall Source
Maha Season September to March North-East Monsoon
Yala Season May to August South-West Monsoon

Key Cultivation Zones

  • Dry Zone: Anuradhapura, Polonnaruwa (Major production areas).
  • Wet Zone: Kalutara, Galle (Rain-fed cultivation).
  • Intermediate Zone: Kurunegala (Mixed climate).
