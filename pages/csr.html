Community Impact

Empowering Rural Sri Lanka.

Digital Literacy for Farmers

We conduct weekend workshops in Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa to teach elderly farmers how to use smartphones for weather tracking and disease scanning.

Sustainable Soil Program

AgriCare distributes free organic compost guides to reduce the dependency on imported chemical fertilizers, protecting our soil for future generations.

Youth in Agriculture

We provide internships for university students from Peradeniya and Wayamba University to work on AI model training and field data collection.
