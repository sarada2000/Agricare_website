Our Story

AgriCare began with a simple observation: Crop diseases in Sri Lanka are often detected too late.

Traditional farming relies on manual inspection, which can be slow and inaccurate. We realized that by bridging the gap between agriculture and Artificial Intelligence, we could put the power of a laboratory into the hands of every farmer with a smartphone.
