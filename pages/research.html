AI Research & Methodology

The Technology

AgriCare uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) based on the ResNet-50 architecture. This deep learning model processes leaf images to identify texture patterns associated with fungal and viral infections.

The Dataset

Our model was trained on a proprietary dataset of 12,500 labeled images collected from paddy fields in the Dry Zone of Sri Lanka.
*Dataset validated by agricultural officers.
